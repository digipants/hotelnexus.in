ANSWERS TO COMMON QUESTIONS

How far is the Hotel from Charbagh Railway Station?
Hotel Nexus is located approximately 500 m from Charbagh Railway Station.
What are the tourist attractions around?
Our Hotel is surrounded by multiple tourist attractions which include Temples, Historic Monuments & Various Shopping Hubs.
How can I get from the Lucknow Airport to the Hotel?
You can book a taxi at your own OR can call us to book a taxi on your behalf. We provide this service at very nominal cost.

Yes off course. All our guests are precious to us. We encourage and welcome our foreign guests to stay with us.

You can always call us or join our Social Media pages to always get latest updates and offers!
