OUR STORY

Hotel Nexus is one of the Best Luxury Hotels in Lucknow, located in the exclusive area of Lucknow, less than 1 km from Charbagh Railway Station and 13 kms from the Airport and just 4 km away from Heart of the city Hazratganj. Hotel Nexus is one of the leading Hotels in Charbagh Lucknow for serving Pure Vegetarian cuisines. The aromatic taste of our cuisines is our uniqueness. Today the whole world around us is turning into Vegetarian as it is more healthier, hygienic and organic. We believe in the same by offering Pure Vegetarian Multi Cuisine which will attract you in all sense. The strategic location accompanied by plush ambience, makes the hotel the best destination for corporate and leisure travelers to have the feel of comfort in business capital. Our 32 fully appointed spacious rooms and suites have been thoughtfully designed keeping in mind all the modern day luxuries. Our every room is poised to cater to your every need, when away from home. From LED TV, high speed internet connectivity to intuitive room service, our hospitality is tuned to ensure that your stay with us is truly refreshing.
